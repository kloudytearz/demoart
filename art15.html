Love of Winter
George Wesley Bellows
1914
Oil on canvas
81.6 × 101.6 cm (32 1/2 × 40 1/2 in.)

Inspired by a February 1914 blizzard, George Bellows painted Love of Winter to capture the energy of a snowy New York City scene. Using vibrant colors and dynamic brushstrokes, he depicted a diverse group of skaters and onlookers, reflecting the city's varied social classes enjoying public parks and winter leisure.
