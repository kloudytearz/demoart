Nightlife
Archibald John Motley Jr.
1943
Oil on canvas
91.4 × 121.3 cm (36 × 47 3/4 in.)

Archibald Motley’s Nightlife captures the vibrant energy of a crowded Chicago cabaret with dynamic figures, diagonal lines, and vivid magenta and violet tones. Inspired by Edward Hopper’s Nighthawks, Motley uses artificial lighting to highlight social interactions and the lively atmosphere of the scene.
