A Sunday on La Grande Jatte — 1884
Georges Seurat
1884-1886
Oil on canvas
207.5 × 308.1 cm (81 3/4 × 121 1/4 in.)

Georges Seurat's A Sunday on La Grande Jatte depicts people from various social classes in a Paris park, blending modern life with timelessness inspired by ancient art. Painted using pointillism, Seurat applied small dots of color that merge into vibrant, harmonious forms when viewed from a distance.
