Paris Street; Rainy Day
Gustave Caillebotte
1877
Oil on canvas
212.2 × 276.2 cm (83 1/2 × 108 3/4 in.); Framed: 241.3 × 306.1 × 10.2 cm (95 × 120 1/2 × 4 in.)

Gustave Caillebotte’s Paris Street; Rainy Day captures the changing urban landscape of late 19th-century Paris, showing modernity through life-sized figures and an innovative perspective. The painting’s bold composition and contemporary subject matter made it a standout at the 1877 Impressionist exhibition, foreshadowing later works like Seurat’s A Sunday on La Grande Jatte.
